# General Parking Information

AutoPark City Center is a multi-level parking facility serving the downtown district.
The garage has 200 covered spaces across two zones (Zone A and Zone B), including
accessible spaces near every elevator and electric-vehicle charging bays on level 1.

The facility is staffed during working hours and monitored by CCTV around the clock.
Lost tickets can be resolved at the payment office on the ground floor. Oversized
vehicles and vans are accommodated in Zone B, which has a higher clearance of 2.4 metres.

For general enquiries, drivers can ask this assistant about working hours, prices,
live space availability, the facility location, and how to make a reservation.
