# Parking Rules and Policies

- Display your ticket or reservation reference on the dashboard where requested.
- Park within the marked bays only. Do not block ramps, fire exits, or accessible bays.
- Accessible bays require a valid disabled-parking permit.
- Electric-vehicle charging bays are for vehicles that are actively charging.
- The maximum vehicle height is 2.0 metres in Zone A and 2.4 metres in Zone B.
- The facility accepts card and contactless payment; cash is accepted at the office.
- Vehicles left beyond their paid or reserved period may incur an overstay charge.

The facility is not liable for items left inside vehicles. Please lock your car and take
valuables with you.
