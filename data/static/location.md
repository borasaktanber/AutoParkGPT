# Location and Directions

AutoPark City Center is located at 14 Market Street, in the heart of the downtown
district, next to the Central Library. The entrance for vehicles is on Market Street;
the pedestrian exit leads directly onto the main shopping promenade.

By car: from the ring road, take exit 5 (City Center) and follow signs for "Market
Street Parking". By public transport: the garage is a three-minute walk from Central
Station and is served by bus routes 2, 9, and 14, which stop on Library Square.

The accessible entrance and the electric-vehicle charging bays are on level 1, reachable
directly from the Market Street ramp.
