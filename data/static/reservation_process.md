# How to Make a Reservation

You can reserve a parking space directly with this assistant. To create a reservation we
collect four details: your first name, your last name, your car (licence plate) number,
and your reservation period (a start and an end date and time).

The assistant will guide you through any missing details one step at a time. Once all
details are provided, the reservation is created with the status "pending approval" and
sent to a parking administrator for review. You will be notified once it is approved or
rejected.

Reservations can be made for any period up to 30 days long and must start in the future.
If you need to change a reservation, contact the payment office with your reservation
reference.
